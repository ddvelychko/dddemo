RULES:

When it comes time to play, you and your opponent will choose from one of the three objects —
either rock, paper or scissors. Then will be named a winner based on which objects you played.
Every game will be different,so stay alert! Three rounds will be played.

Determine the winner.

After you’ve both played an object, look to see who’s won this round. Each object wins against
one shape and loses to another. For instance, rock “crushes” scissors but is “covered” by paper,
paper “covers” rock but is “cut” by scissors, and scissors is “crushed” by rock but “cuts” paper.
The player who picks the stronger of the two objects is the winner of the round. If both players
throw the same object, it’s a tie.

When three games are played, it will be calculated a result.
